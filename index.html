FAQ

USDI is a fully reserve-backed stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar, designed for secure payments, DeFi integration, and cross-chain usage.

Each USDI token is backed 1:1 by reserves including cash equivalents and low-risk financial instruments, with regular transparency reporting.

USDI is available on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20), TRON (TRC-20), and Solana, with future expansion to Ethereum and other major networks.

Users can earn through staking, liquidity provision, and participation in loyalty programs and ecosystem rewards.

USDI can be redeemed for equivalent value through supported platforms and partners, subject to availability and compliance requirements.

The protocol prioritizes security through audited smart contracts, reserve transparency, and risk management systems, though all digital assets carry inherent risks.

Minimal fees may apply for minting, redemption, or network transactions, depending on the platform and blockchain used.

USDI combines full reserve backing, multi-chain availability, staking rewards, and a loyalty ecosystem, creating both stability and utility.
